Smart Home

This project is to simulate a smart hub for a house. This hub is able to remotely control temperture and lights, and also monitor a home with sensors.

Features

SMART THERMOSTAT

Control your thermostat from anywhere or have it automatically manage the temperature of your home.

WEATHER

Know the weather outside before you leave your home.

SMART LIGHTING

Control your lighting in your home from where ever you are.

POWER MANAGEMENT

Know how much power you're consuming and manage the power of your home.
